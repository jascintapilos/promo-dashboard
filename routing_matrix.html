Self-Serve Brands (4 Total)

✅ You can assign directly:

  • WS1 & WS2: Via CRM/Smartico (FastTrack for WS1)
  • QPRO1 & QP2A: Via BO Dashboard

You have direct access, authority, and control. No waiting. Assign immediately.

Promo Team Routes (26 Brands)

❌ Promo team assigns these:

  • QPRO2–19: 17 other QPRO brands (not QPRO1)
  • QP2B/C/D: 3 other QP2 merchants (not QP2A)

Post in #ba-promo with player name, tier, desired bonus. Promo team assigns in 1–2 hours.

Request Format for #ba-promo

🎯 BONUS ASSIGNMENT REQUEST Brand: [Brand code, e.g., QPRO5] Player Name/ID: [Player identifier] Player Tier: [Bronze/Silver/Gold/Platinum/Diamond] Desired Bonus: [Code or description, e.g., FT_DEP_VIP_100 or "100% deposit bonus up to SGD 500"] Urgency: Regular [or HIGH if time-sensitive]

Decision Tree: "Where Do I Send This?"

  1. What's the brand?
    • WS1 or WS2? → Assign directly in CRM (Self-serve) ✅
    • QPRO1 or QP2A? → Assign directly in BO (Self-serve) ✅
    • QPRO2–19 or QP2B/C/D? → Post in #ba-promo (Promo team, 1–2 hrs) ❌
    • Other? → Ask Jascinta first ❓
  2. Do I have the promo code?
    • YES → Check Weekly Promo List, follow brand SOP, assign
    • NO → Ask in #ba-promo for the code
  3. Is this self-serve or promo team?
    • Self-serve → Assign now
    • Promo team → Post in #ba-promo, wait 1–2 hours

Key Rule

If a brand is NOT in the self-serve list (WS1, WS2, QPRO1, QP2A), you CANNOT assign directly.

Stop and post in #ba-promo. Promo team will handle it.
