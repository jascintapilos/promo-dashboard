Promo team · root cause analysis · Jan–Jun 2026
45 of 81 escalations (56%) are preventable promo team errors. The remaining 44% are platform or system limitations outside the team's control. Three recurring patterns — T&C content mismatches, missing providers, and unlinked member groups — account for 34 of those 45 cases (76%) and are each fixable with a checklist step.
19 T&C / content mismatch 9 wrong BO setting 8 provider / game not added 7 member group missing 2 unclassified HE
HE rate has held at 7–8 cases/month since March — not declining without process change

Root cause & recommended fixes

T&C ↔ BO alignment
Largest single category · 42% of all HE cases
19 cases
  • 5×Claim window: T&C says 30 days, BO set to 7 days — repeated May–Jun cluster
  • 5×Turnover or bonus amount differs between T&C text and BO value (e.g. ×5 UI vs ×20 T&C)
  • 5×Game/provider restriction stated in T&C does not match BO eligible list
  • 4×Wrong locale, missing promo name, or round count differs from title
Fix: pre-publish T&C cross-check For every promo, map each claim in the T&C to its exact BO field: reward_validity (claim window), turnover multiplier, eligible providers, and bonus cap. Any figure in the T&C must have a matching BO setting verified before activation.
Provider & game checklist
Always caught post-escalation, never pre-launch
8 cases
  • 4×FS game provider blacklisted in error or never added to eligible list — member plays, TO doesn't count
  • 3×Category (e.g. Live Casino) not added to BO despite being named in T&C or campaign
  • 1×Game name field left blank in FS promo — member claims but receives no spins
Fix: eligible provider verification step Before activating any promo: confirm every provider/game named in the T&C or campaign brief is in the BO eligible list and not on the blacklist. For FS promos, also confirm the game name field is populated.
Member group as final step
Jun spike: 4 of 7 total cases in one month
7 cases
  • 4×June 2026 cluster — QPRO3, QPRO2 (×2), QP2C: promo live but member group never linked
  • 3×Jan–Feb cases: member group omitted on multi-merchant promos (QP2A/C/D)
  • allAlways discovered only when a member tries to claim and is blocked
Fix: member group as final activation gate Add "Link member group" as the explicit last step in the promo activation SOP — completed after all settings are verified, not at creation. For multi-merchant promos, verify each merchant's group separately.
